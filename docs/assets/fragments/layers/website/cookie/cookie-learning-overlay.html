What are cookies?

Cookies and similar technologies are small data tools stored or read by a website to remember selected states, preserve security-sensitive continuity, and support core interface behavior during and across visits.

They do not all serve the same purpose

Some are strictly necessary for security, session continuity, consent integrity, and accessibility. Others may support analytics or a more refined experience only when you allow them.

They can remember selected states

For example, they may preserve language choices, maintain protected-session continuity, or retain privacy preferences so the website behaves consistently.

Optional cookies remain your decision

Neuroartan separates strictly necessary cookies from optional categories so you can review, allow, or decline non-essential use with greater clarity.

Why we use them

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep Neuroartan secure, preserve essential functionality, respect your saved consent state, and, where you permit it, understand performance and improve continuity.

Security and integrity

Some cookies help defend the website against abuse, preserve request integrity, and maintain the trustworthiness of critical flows.

Core continuity

Some cookies preserve session continuity, consent choices, and essential interface behavior so the website can operate as expected.

Optional improvement insights

Where you allow it, optional cookies can help us understand performance, identify friction, and refine the experience without being required for the website to function.
